Cookie Privacy

Last updated: February 2026

Cookie Notice

FreshCart uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze website traffic, and personalize content. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and your choices regarding their use.

Article 1

What Are Cookies?

1.1

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit websites.

1.2

They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time.

1.3

Cookies can be "session" cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent" cookies (remain until they expire or are deleted).

Article 2

Types of Cookies We Use

2.1

Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security.

2.2

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings.

2.3

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website.

2.4

Marketing Cookies: Track browsing habits to deliver personalized advertisements.

Article 3

How We Use Cookies

3.1

To authenticate users and maintain secure sessions.

3.2

To remember items in your shopping cart and wishlist.

3.3

To analyze website performance and improve user experience.

3.4

To deliver relevant product recommendations and promotions.

3.5

To prevent fraudulent activities and enhance security measures.

Article 4

Third-Party Cookies

4.1

We use Google Analytics to track website traffic and user behavior.

4.2

Social media platforms (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter) may set cookies for sharing content.

4.3

Payment processors use cookies to securely process transactions.

4.4

Advertising partners may use cookies to deliver targeted advertisements.

Article 5

Cookie Duration

5.1

Session Cookies: Last only for your current browsing session and expire when you close your browser.

5.2

Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years).

5.3

You can manually delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.

Article 6

Managing Cookies

6.1

You can adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time.

6.2

Most browsers accept cookies by default, but you can modify this in privacy settings.

6.3

Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality and user experience.

6.4

You can opt-out of personalized advertising through platforms like YourOnlineChoices (EU) or NAI (US).

Article 7

Cookie Consent

7.1

Upon your first visit, we display a cookie banner requesting your consent.

7.2

You can accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences.

7.3

Your consent preferences are stored for future visits.

7.4

You can change your cookie preferences at any time through our cookie settings panel.

Article 8

Updates & Contact

8.1

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology or regulations.

8.2

Significant changes will be notified via website notice or email.

8.3

For questions about this Cookie Policy, contact us at cookies@freshcart.com
